Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2024

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide useful information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Performance Cookies: Improve website speed and performance

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They help our website remember your choices during your visit.

3.2 Persistent Cookies

These remain on your device until they expire or you delete them. They help us remember your preferences for future visits.

3.3 Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. These are used for analytics and advertising purposes.

4. Cookie Management

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Modify your browser settings to accept or reject cookies
  • Delete cookies that have already been stored on your device
  • Use private browsing mode to prevent cookie storage

Please note that blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience on our website.
